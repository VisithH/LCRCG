Who Are We?

A Non-profit Charity organization

We are a school-based Non Profit Leo Club established in Leo District 306 A2 in 2016. It is sponsored by the Lions Club of Karapitiya. We are actively serving the community over the past years with the contribution of multi-talented Leos and executive officers. Activities conducted by Centennial Leo Club of Richmond College include Childcare, Eldercare, Sports, Environmental, Healthcare, Leadership Development Projects, and many more.

How all of it Started

The journey of the Centennial Leo Club of Richmond College began on 01st of July 2015 with fifty members. The Club was in motion with the guidance of our staff advisors and leo advisor while being sponsored by Lions Club of Karapitiya. Currently Leo Club of Centennial leo Club of Richmond College has more than 40 member and we always strengthen the Leo District 306 A2 on our utmost level and it paid off as the Centennial Leo Club of Richmond College became the best leo club in Sri Lanka taking the our club and the Leo District 306 A2 into glory. The Past Club President was Leo Kulith Sathnidu and the Past Club Secretary was Leo Dumindu De Silva
